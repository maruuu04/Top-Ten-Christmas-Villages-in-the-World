Grindelwald, Switzerland

The village of Grindelwald sits at the foot of two dramatic peaks of the Swiss Alps – it’s so picturesque that it’s been used as the setting of a number of films, including “The Golden Compass.” This notable winter resort destination stands apart from the rest due to its especially charming atmosphere during the holidays – so much so that it just might make you believe in Santa Claus again. You’ll find beautiful decorations lining the streets and massive Christmas trees around every corner, along with mulled wine and plenty of goods to purchase at the Grindelwald market stands.
Nestled in the region of Bernese Oberland, Grindelwald is the perfect destination for a magical Christmas. Surrounded by the majestic Eiger mountain and offering spectacular views, this charming village captivates visitors with its beauty and festive atmosphere. One of the highlights of Grindelwald is to ride on the renowned Jungfrau Railway. This iconic train takes you to the Jungfraujoch, the highest railway station in Europe, where you can marvel at the stunning panorama of the Aletsch Glacier. The journey itself is a “once in a lifetime” experience, passing through tunnels and offering glimpses of Switzerland’s alpine wonderland. The streets of Grindelwald are littered with sparkling lights, and the air is filled with the aroma of spiced fruit and delicious treats. Explore the market stalls to find exceptional gifts, crafts, and local delicacies. Immerse yourself in the festive spirit as you stroll through the village, taking in the atmosphere.

San Fernando Pampanga, Philippines

Located in the lively city of San Fernando, Pampanga, the Christmas Village Pampanga is a cherished holiday destination that embodies the spirit of Christmas in a fun, family-friendly environment. Every year, this charming village turns into a magical place filled with lights, decorations, and festive displays, attracting visitors from across the Philippines. Renowned for its stunning lights, themed exhibits, and vibrant holiday atmosphere, it has become one of the top Christmas villages to explore in Pampanga.
San Fernando in Pampanga takes pride in its name as the Christmas Capital of the Philippines. Every Christmas season, thousand of locals and tourist head to the city to witness one of the grandest festivals in the country, the Giant Lantern Festival. Locally known as the Ligligan Parul, the festival brings out the locals' competitive spirit as they compete for the best giant parol. Since its starts in 1908, this event has been held every December, with barangays competing for the title of "San-Fernando's Greatest Lantern-maker." This makes the city one of the best Christmas Villages in the Philippines.
Showcasing the rich Kapampangan culture, the Christmas Village Pampanga offers more than just beautiful sights. It encourages families and friends to dive into traditional Christmas celebrations, engage in enjoyable activities, and make lasting memories together. For those wanting to feel the joy of the season, this village provides a unique holiday experience that is both welcoming and heartwarming.
